# Contributing

Predictive History welcomes focused public contributions that improve `ph-civ` and `ph-apo`: study orientation, data quality, validation, public navigation, and commentary canvas development.

Do not add transcripts, full commentary bodies, private notes, or unsourced claims. Keep contributions provider-neutral and source-grounded.

Do not add large media binaries directly to Git. This repo tracks transcripts, commentaries, cards, routes, schemas, and small generated materials. Large archives stay outside Git.

Retired museum layer: `docs/archive/ph-mus-retired.md`.
