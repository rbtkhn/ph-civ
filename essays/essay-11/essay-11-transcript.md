Conspiracy theories swirl, and the authorities release agonizing drips of information. Bullet casings are engraved with ideological messaging. The perpetrator is captured, and the spotlight focuses squarely on him. The culprit is a hero to some, and a villain to others. He is a mystery and a cipher. A star student from a good family who somehow became radicalized, and disappeared into his hatred and anger.
