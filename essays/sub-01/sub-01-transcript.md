# Vision, Mission, Goals

## Part I: Full transcript

My name is Professor Jiang, and I teach Beijing high school students. You may have seen my YouTube videos, in which I teach world history and geo-politics. I’ve been uploading my lectures for over a year now, and I hope to do so until the end of my life. (Or the end of the world.)

If you’ve watched any of my YouTube lectures, you will know that I’m a passionate educator. I’m animated by a desire to teach students how to think for themselves. I love it when my students ask questions, and challenge me. That means they’re paying attention, and enjoying the class. That also means that I am forced to think more deeply, and teach more clearly.

If you are a subscriber to my YouTube channel Predictive History https://www.youtube.com/@PredictiveHistory, you will know that I am attempting to start an intellectual movement to reform how history is taught. Whenever we teach history, we are teaching a certain conception and framework of the world. From these distinct values and assumptions, we can construct an analytical model that is also a predictive model. If a historical understanding is correct, then it must accomplish three objectives:

· Connect the past

· Explain the present

· Predict the future

By using these three criteria, we can evaluate all analytical models, and slowly and collectively build a model that best explains the trajectory of human history. If you are a fan of Isaac Asimov, you will know he calls this model “psycho-history.”

For the past year, I have been studying and teaching world history in order to construct this analytical model, and this model is already yelling something disturbing. The world is ending, and it will end very soon. There is nothing we can do to stop it.

There is hope though. We must remember that history is determined by those who are willing to fight and die for what they believe in.

We can choose to build a better world after the apocalypse. And we have no choice but to believe we can do so. Or to be more precise I have no choice. I have three young children, and that is why I choose to fight for a new world built on the ashes of this one.

For my three young children, I commit myself to building a movement that will prepare for this new world. This substack is an integral part of my ambition, and it has three core goals:

Supplement my teaching. I was surprised by how many viewers of my YouTube geo-politics and world history lectures asked for supplementary reading material. In the 2025-2026 academic year, I’ll be three teaching three new classes: The Secret History of the World (How Power Works), The Great Books, and Game Theory Analysis. Each lecture will come with an essay that will offer references, notes, and recommending readings.

Reveal the future. As my YouTube subscribers will know, I have made predictions for how the world ends. These predictions include an American ground invasion of Iran, a Russia-NATO showdown in Odessa, and the destruction of the Al-Aqsa Mosque. With my substack, I will be tracking and analyzing world events. I will attempt to explain why things are happening the way they are, and how they tie into the larger picture.

Plan the movement. I will also discuss how we can build a community. We already have a strong YouTube channel and a vibrant Discord community (https://discord.gg/QtFKwTGMVn). In these early days, much of my work will be theoretical and strategizing. I am currently working on a book proposal. I am attempting to spread my ideas far and wide by appearing on other YouTube channels. I hope to do a weekly livestream soon. In the future, I hope to do lectures and workshops around the world. Ideally, I’d like to build schools as the foundations of the Predictive History global intellectual movement.

The world is ending. The journey to re-build it starts here and now.
