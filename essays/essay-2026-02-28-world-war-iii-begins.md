# World War III Begins

## Part I: Full transcript

At around eight in the morning on Saturday, the Americans and Israelis bombarded Iran with missiles, aiming for the Ayatollah Khamenei and President Masoud Pezeshkian. The Israelis claimed to have killed Khamenei, but this is yet to be confirmed. Among the first confirmed casualties in this war are 51 girls when their elementary school in southern Iran was bombed.

In a video speech, President Trump announced that America was intent on overthrowing the Iranian government, which he blamed for sponsoring terrorism and suppressing the Iranian people. He encouraged the Revolutionary Guard Corps to surrender, and the Iranian people to rise up. He warned that there would be American casualties.

As promised, Iran retaliated by setting the whole region on fire. Its missiles targeted Israel, as well as the GCC countries of Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, and Bahrain. The Houthis have joined the battle, and closed off the Red Sea to shipping. America is bombing the Shia militias in Iraq, and Israel is bombing Hezbollah in Lebanon.

American officials have stated that Operation Epic Fury will be a four-day operation, which means that it should end on Tuesday March 3rd. That coincides with the Jewish holiday of Purim when Israel celebrates how Jews rallied together to foil an extermination plot in ancient Persia, and killed 75,000 of their enemies (as recounted in the Book of Esther). That day is also when a Blood Moon will appear. Freemasons (who control America’s national security apparatus) revere the number “33.”

There is a good mundane reason why the Americans have planned for a four-day operation. It was reported that chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Dan Caine estimates that the American navy and air force have munitions only for four days.

Trump likes quick and easy wars in which he can declare victory. The hope is that after four days both the Iranians and the Americans will exhaust themselves, and they’ll be forced to negotiate a lasting peace. But as Mike Tyson once said, “Everyone has a plan until they get punched in the face.”

Why would the Iranians settle for another “peace”? After the 12-Day War, Trump declared that the Iranian nuclear weapons program had been “obliterated,” and therefore America had no reason to ever attack Iran again. Netanyahu asked Putin to re-assure Tehran that Israel would honor the peace.

There are also domestic considerations. The Americans refuse to lift economic sanctions, and droughts in Iran worsen year by year. A war would rally the Iranian people, as well as Shia Muslims across the Middle East (especially if Khamenei is confirmed dead).

Yes, the Iranians are impoverished, but luxury has corrupted the Americans. The super-carriers Gerald Ford and Abraham Lincoln are mighty, but American soldiers are so fattened by “surf and turf” Friday dinners that they complain about broken toilets — how would their psychologies fare against hyper-sonic missiles?

As is their custom, the Americans would seek victory on the cheap. They would avoid ground troops, and seek to inflict as much damage as possible from a safe distance. This means that as this war progresses the Americans shift from striking high-value military targets (which are hidden in mountains or bunkers) to critical civilian infrastructure (water and electricity). The faith is that hardship would drive the Iranian people into open rebellion. But the Iranians are not Americans, and Shia Muslims would more likely embrace martyrdom (as was the case in the apocalyptic Iran-Iraq War).

The Iranians would not permit the Americans an easy off-ramp. Shielded within their mountain fortress, the Iranians could destroy the energy infrastructure of GCC countries, and close off the Strait of Hormuz. Given that they are essentially oil-fields that can be lit on fire by Iranian drones, the GCC countries will either have to bribe the Iranians for an uneasy peace, or pay American ground troops to invade Iran. That is the trap that Iran is slowly setting for Trump.

America’s erstwhile ally Israel is also interested in a protracted war that would replace Pax Americana with Pax Judaica. What would happen if a hyper-sonic were to sink the Abraham Lincoln, which is just sitting in the Persian Gulf waiting to be attacked? Yes, the Iranians have reportedly purchased “carrier-killer” missiles from the Chinese — but could anyone be sure who fired the missiles if the super-carrier were to sink? Remember the Maine?

Americans would cry for blood. Congress would authorize Trump to declare war, and with his emergency war powers he would no longer have to worry about the mid-term elections.

Can’t the Democrats see this from a mile away? The Washington elite wants war with Iran while covering their ass. The only time that the Democrats and Republicans all cheered during Trump’s State of the Union speech on Tuesday was when he declared he would never allow Iran to develop a nuclear weapon. Legislation to demand that Trump seek Congressional approval before attacking Iran was conveniently delayed to next Tuesday. Somehow a war that seventy percent of Americans oppose are supported by almost all of Congress — a problem that demands a false flag.

And this is how we have stumbled onto World War Three. William Butler Yeats said it best:

Things fall apart; the center cannot hold;

Mere anarchy is loosed upon the world,

The blood-dimmed tide is loosed, and everywhere

The ceremony of innocence is drowned;

The best lack all conviction, while the worst

Are full of passionate intensity.
