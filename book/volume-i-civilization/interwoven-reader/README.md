# Volume I: Interwoven Civilization Spine

This is the canonical reader-order surface for Volume I.

Read Volume I here first: as one civilizational sequence whose main line is the `civ-*` series, with Great Books evidence and selected Secret History support woven in where they materially deepen the chapter at hand.

The packet shelves remain below this layer:

- [Civilization packet shelf](../civilization-spine/README.md)
- [Great Books evidence shelf](../great-books-evidence/README.md)
- [Secret History support shelf](../secret-history-support/README.md)
- [Routes](../routes/README.md)
- [Volume I Parts (doorways)](../parts/README.md)

## Reading Law

- `civ-*` remains the chronological and argumentative backbone.
- `gb-*` appears as civilizational evidence, not as a coequal parallel pillar.
- selected `sh-*` appears only by function: `prologue`, `deepening`, or `hinge`.
- Secret History support is bounded; it is not a hidden third Volume I spine.
- support entries may recur where the real topical weave requires it.
