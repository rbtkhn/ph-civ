# Interviews #16: Diary of a CEO (Steven Bartlett) — World War 3 Is About To Begin

## Core Strategic Thesis

Long-form **Diary of a CEO** studio interview (Steven Bartlett host; Jiang Xueqin guest): recap of three 2024 predictions (Trump win, US war on Iran, US loses and reshapes order); **eight new predictions** (Trump third term, US–China grand bargain, Iran forever war + national draft, AI surveillance state, Greater Israel, NATO–Russia fight for Odessa, East Asia flashpoints); chess-set grand-strategy comparison (US, Russia, Israel, Iran); National Defense Strategy read as imperial roadmap; Strait of Hormuz / Kharg Island / Caspian reinforcement; Plato **Allegory of the Cave** extended to financial elite / media / independent media tension; closing on love, family, and creative mission.

## Defined Terms (speaker usage)

- **Game theory** — nation-states as players optimizing under rules and incentives.
- **Grand strategy** — chess king/queen/rook/bishop/knight/pawn mapping per power.
- **Shock and awe / decapitation** — regime-leadership strike doctrine (Iraq vs Iran topography contrast).
- **Mosaic strategy** — decentralized provincial command in Iran.
- **Shadow fleet** — sanctioned Russian tanker network as naval workaround.
- **Greater Israel Project** — biblical Nile-to-Euphrates territorial frame (speaker usage).
- **Third Rome** — Russian civilizational grand strategy (Dugin reference).
- **Hermetic philosophy / Allegory of the Cave** — layered “shadow” reality and financial-media stack.

## Claims, Forecasts, And Falsifiers

1. US had structural incentive to attack Iran to defend petrodollar / reserve-currency system.
2. US likely loses **ground-war** definition of Iran war (political will, manufacturing, attrition in mountain fortress).
3. **80–90%** probability speaker assigns to WWIII-scale expansion once Iran war dynamics persist.
4. Russia likely enters on Iran’s side (Caspian reinforcement, financing, possible nuclear umbrella framing).
5. Trump third term plausible via Don Jr. / VP succession loophole or emergency war-powers election delay (speaker frame; legally contested).
6. US–China “grand bargain” likely (Treasuries vs energy/market access); China triangulates not full belligerent.
7. US national draft auto-registration for males 18–24 from December (speaker claim; verify against statute).
8. World moves toward AI-linked digital ID / currency surveillance (China model cited).
9. Israel positioned to absorb CENTCOM role after US Middle East exit (Greater Israel frame).
10. NATO and Russia fight for **Odessa** as Ukraine war terminal prize (grain / Black Sea control).
11. East Asia flashpoints: Taiwan–Japan, Strait of Malacca, North Korea extortion of Seoul.
12. American empire may collapse within **5–10 years** (debt, overstretch, global backlash) — Bronze Age collapse analogy.
13. Plato cave / private bankers / media as shadow-projectors; independent media threatens edifice stability.
