# Game Theory #5: The World Game

## Where This Sits

This chapter is one of the clearest Civilization-side anchors in the Game Theory sequence. It builds a general model of world history around civilizational rise and fall, making it a direct law-discovery bridge into the larger Predictive History project.

## Reading Posture

Read it as a structural chapter about what makes societies strong or weak over time. Its value is not current-event commentary but the attempt to name recurring laws of world order.

## Historical Pressure Points

- rise and fall as a patterned civilizational process
- energy, openness, and cohesion as recurring strength variables
- world order as a changing game rather than a permanent equilibrium
