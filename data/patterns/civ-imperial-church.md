# Imperial Church

## Summary

Spiritual authority and imperial administration can fuse into a durable governing system, producing both cohesion and later rupture pressure.

## Strategy Use

Use when state authority, religious legitimacy, obedience, and institutional scale reinforce one another or become contested together.

## Source Cards

- `civ-27`
- `civ-40`

## Limits

Keep the pattern tied to visible institutions and claims of authority. Do not use it as a generic church-state label.
