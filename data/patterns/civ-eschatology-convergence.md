# Eschatology Convergence

## Summary

Different actors can bring incompatible end-time, destiny, or sacred-history frameworks into the same strategic arena.

## Strategy Use

Use when competing religious or ideological time horizons shape alliance behavior, risk tolerance, or claims about historical necessity.

## Source Cards

- `geo-19`

## Limits

This pattern is especially easy to overstate. Use it only when the relevant actors or movements make explicit sacred-history claims.
