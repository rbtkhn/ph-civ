# Chokepoint Pressure

## Summary

Geography, infrastructure, energy, shipping, and deterrence can turn a local conflict into a system-wide pressure field.

## Strategy Use

Use for Hormuz, maritime corridors, energy routes, nuclear infrastructure, proxy platforms, or other strategic bottlenecks where incentives and physical routes interact.

## Source Cards

- `geo-14`
- `gt-16`

## Limits

Do not infer closure, blockade, or escalation from geography alone. Use current primary evidence for live operational claims.
