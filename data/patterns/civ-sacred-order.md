# Sacred Order As Architecture

## Summary

A civilization can turn moral, theological, and imaginative order into a navigable public architecture that tells people where they stand.

## Strategy Use

Use when analyzing legitimacy systems that organize guilt, authority, hierarchy, punishment, aspiration, or civilizational purpose.

## Source Cards

- `civ-29`
- `gb-09`

## Limits

This is not a claim that all legitimacy is religious. It marks cases where sacred or moral architecture visibly structures public order.
