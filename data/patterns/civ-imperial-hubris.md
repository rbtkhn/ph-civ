# Imperial Hubris

## Summary

An empire can mistake its inherited reach for permanent capacity, overreading compliance and underreading local resistance or system cost.

## Strategy Use

Use when strategy-codex needs a public frame for overextension, failed coercion, reputation loss, or mismatch between imperial narrative and material capacity.

## Source Cards

- `geo-03`
- `geo-06`

## Limits

The pattern does not decide whether an actor is declining. It asks what evidence supports or weakens the overreach claim.
