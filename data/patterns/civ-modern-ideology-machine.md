# Modern Ideology Machine

## Summary

Modernity can convert philosophy, class, race, science, empire, technology, and administration into mass ideological systems.

## Strategy Use

Use when an analysis needs a public frame for ideological machinery, administrative abstraction, mass mobilization, or modern claims to historical inevitability.

## Source Cards

- `civ-56`
- `civ-58`

## Limits

Do not treat modern ideology as one thing. The pattern points to machinery and scale, not a single doctrine or moral verdict.
