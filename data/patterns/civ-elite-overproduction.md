# Elite Overproduction

## Summary

A social order becomes brittle when status, offices, and elite pathways cannot absorb the number of people trained or positioned to expect them.

## Strategy Use

Use when strategy-codex needs a public frame for status blockage, factional rivalry, or institutional crowding without treating any current case as a direct Bronze Age replay.

## Source Cards

- `civ-06`

## Limits

This pattern is a public orientation frame, not a universal collapse law. Current cases need their own demographic, institutional, and evidentiary checks.
